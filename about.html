About Me

I am a Research Scientist and Data Scientist with expertise in Applied Mathematics, Computational Fluid Dynamics, Turbulence Modeling, and Predictive Analytics.

I completed my PhD in Applied Mathematics (with a Statistics minor) at the University of Wyoming.

I enjoy combining mathematics, modeling, and programming to solve problems in engineering, finance, gaming analytics, cyber-forensics, and research.
